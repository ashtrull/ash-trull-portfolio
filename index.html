About

I am a community organizer, full stack web developer, and facilitator committed to the pursuit of justice in all its many forms: social, racial, economic, and environmental. I use my skills in systems thinking from community organizing to develop web applications, build political campaigns, and manage projects to solve the greatest challenges of our time. It is my passion as a facilitator to coordinate groups of people in finding shared purpose and goals, then creating a road map for getting there.

My core values are community power, democracy, equity, autonomy, and liberation. My latest work has been adding web development to my toolbox for social change, and I am excited to bring my skills as a developer to social change work. When I'm not coding, I'm also working as a co-op consultant to help experienced and emerging worker cooperatives, and organizing more white people to join a multiracial movement for racial justice through Showing Up for Racial Justice RI, and cultivating my backyard farm.
